Strong Mentors, Strong Culture About In Your Shoes

Our founders, Ari and Owen, were discussing their experiences applying to college and realized they were speaking completely different languages. Since they came from disparate parts of the country with different levels of privilege, each was on his own particular playing field for the transition to college. They founded In Your Shoes to level that playing field and provide affordable insight into how to succeed during the next phase of your life.

As the COVID-19 pandemic touches everyoneâ€™s lives, the transition to college is becoming a whole lot harder. On top of uncertainties about making friends and getting involved on campus, high school students are worried about potentially taking classes online and missing the classic college experience. In Your Shoes is here to help during these trying times and into the future.

You will walk out of your advising session feeling better-prepared to make the transition to college than when you walked in.
